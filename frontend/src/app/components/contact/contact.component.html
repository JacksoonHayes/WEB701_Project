Frequently Asked Questions

Grow & Give is a charity platform that enables donors to list items they wish to give away, allowing beneficiaries to redeem these listings using vouchers.

Beneficiaries receive a set amount of vouchers upon registering. These vouchers can be used to redeem items listed by donors on the platform.

If you're a registered donor, you can create listings from your account dashboard. Simply provide details of the items you wish to donate, and beneficiaries will be able to redeem them.

Beneficiaries can browse available listings and use their vouchers to redeem items. Each listing will indicate the voucher amount needed for redemption.

Currently, Grow & Give focuses on item donations through listings. However, if you'd like to support us financially, please contact us for more information on how to help.

Currently, beneficiaries receive a set amount of vouchers upon registration. Additional voucher opportunities may be introduced in the future, so stay tuned!
