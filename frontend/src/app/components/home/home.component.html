About Grow & Give

Welcome to Grow & Give, your go-to platform for fresh and local berries delivered right to your doorstep. Our mission is simple: to connect berry enthusiasts with the finest local berry growers and provide a convenient, delightful shopping experience.

Our Story

Born from a love for farm-fresh produce, Grow & Give was founded with the aim to make local berries more accessible to everyone. We work closely with small, sustainable farms to bring you the freshest, seasonal berries — straight from the field to your home.

Whether you're looking for strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, or something in between, Grow & Give offers a wide range of options. Our flexible subscription plans or one-time purchase options ensure there's something for every berry lover.

How It Works

Grow & Give makes it easy for local farmers and growers to list their fresh produce on our platform. Donors can quickly create listings for berries and other items they wish to share, allowing beneficiaries to redeem vouchers and access quality, local produce.

Beneficiaries can explore the available listings, redeem vouchers, and arrange pick-ups or deliveries of the items they need. This seamless exchange supports the community by reducing food waste and promoting fresh, nutritious options for everyone.
